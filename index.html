Company Overview

Accuracy is an accounting firm that offers bookkeeping, taxes, audits, annual reports and more for a variety of businesses.

Our mission statement is to work hand in hand with businesses to support them financially. To properly excel in this aspect, we have a plan that will assure the gratification of our clients. By precisely choosing and training our employees, we aim to maximize the quality of our company. Through teamwork and communication with our clients, we intend to create the best environment possible. Our professional and meticulous work will produce the best possible outcome for our clients.

Company History

Accuracy is a new and upcoming accounting company that has just launched 2019! However, all of our professional staff has more than enough experience to assist our clients. See staff information below!

President Emily Jones decided to found the company and name it "Accuracy" because it reflects the goals of an excellent accounting firm. Accuracy will create precise and meticulous results for everyon! The company seeks to create the highest standard for their work while listening to the needs of all clients. Though Accuracy is a new company, the expert staff and their dedicated performance in the workplace promises a successful future to come!

Staff Information

Emily Jones, CPA, MBA, PFS President

President Emily Jones graduated as a Certified Public Accountant, Master of Business Administration, and Personal Finance Specialist from Bentley University in 2007. After graduating, she worked at B&L Accounting for 12 years before deciding to branch out on her own. She founded Accuracy to help others with their finances by creating a professional, yet relaxed environment to work with her clients. She and all of the staff seek to carefully ensure customer satisfaction through her company's success.
